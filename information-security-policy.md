# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes the principles and governance requirements for protecting the information assets of EduTrack Learning Solutions. It supports the organization's Information Security Management System (ISMS) and promotes the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organizational information.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, interns, temporary personnel, third-party service providers, and all organizational information assets, including cloud services, business applications, endpoint devices, and supporting infrastructure.

## Policy Objectives

- Protect organizational information assets from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or loss.
- Define clear information security roles and responsibilities.
- Support compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
- Promote a culture of security awareness throughout the organization.
- Encourage continual improvement of the Information Security Management System (ISMS).

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Executive Management

Provide leadership, approve security policies, and allocate resources to support information security.

### Information Security Manager

Maintain the ISMS, coordinate policy reviews, monitor compliance, and report security performance.

### Department Managers

Ensure employees understand and follow security requirements within their departments.

### Employees and Contractors

Protect organizational information, follow approved security procedures, and report security incidents promptly.

## Key Policy Statements

- Information assets shall be identified, classified, and appropriately protected.
- Access to information shall follow the principle of least privilege.
- Cloud services shall be securely configured, monitored, and managed.
- Security incidents shall be reported through approved reporting procedures.
- Security awareness training shall be mandatory for all personnel.
- Information security policies shall be reviewed at least annually.

## Related ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A Controls

- A.5.1 – Policies for Information Security
- A.5.9 – Inventory of Information and Other Associated Assets
- A.5.23 – Information Security for Use of Cloud Services
- A.6.3 – Information Security Awareness, Education and Training
- A.6.8 – Information Security Event Reporting
